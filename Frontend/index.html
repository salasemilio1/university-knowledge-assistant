Getting Started with the University Knowledge Assistant

Hello! 👋 Welcome to the Southwestern University Knowledge Assistant.

This tool is designed to help students navigate their academic journey and make the most of their time on campus. Questions can be asked about specific majors and/or minors, as well as overall degree requirements. Assistance is also available for course planning, tracking graduation progress, and providing information about professors, departments, and academic policies.

If you navigate to the side panel, you will see the following options:

1. Chat: Start a conversation with the chatbot here!

2. Dashboard: View your academic progress. This information is based on what you have filled out in the profile tab.

3. Profile: Here you can fill out general academic information so that you can personalize your experience with the chatbot. Filling out a profile is optional.
