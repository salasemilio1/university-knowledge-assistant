Academic Specialization
Students seeking secondary or all-level certification must complete
24 hours approved by the Education Department in one of the
content areas listed below. Some certification areas include
specific courses that must be included in the 24 hours (see below).
The 24 hours may also be applied toward a major in that area.
(Students completing a major in their content area typically meet
the 24 content hours required for teacher certification.) Students
are strongly advised to consult the catalog for their major and the
Education Department section to determine the professional
development certification courses required.
