## SECTION 1 — METADATA BLOCK

This index covers the Department of Economics and Business at Southwestern University. The department offers two standalone majors (Economics and Business), two paired majors (Economics and Business; Business and Art History), and two minors (Economics and Business). All degrees award the Bachelor of Arts. The department is housed in Mood-Bridwell Hall (MBH) in Georgetown, Texas (512.863.6511). A signature undergraduate program — the Financial Analyst Program (FAP) — allows selected students to manage a real-money investment portfolio as part of SU's endowment. Graduate school partnership pathways exist with Texas A&M University (Mays Business School) and Rice University (MAcc).
