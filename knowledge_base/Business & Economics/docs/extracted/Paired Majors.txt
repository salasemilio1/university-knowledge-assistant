Majoring & Minoring
Students elect to work toward a Bachelor of Arts degree with a major in Economics or Business,
or paired majors in Economics and Business, or Business and Art History. A minor in
Economics, Business or Art History is not allowed with the paired majors in Economics and
Business or Business and Art History.
