Internships
Academic and Summer Internships
A number of academic and summer internships are available in sociology and anthropology.
Students have worked in a variety of setting for internships, including adult and juvenile probation, drug
rehabilitation centers, the Attorney General’s Office, and Texas politicians. Students interested in academic
internships should contact the Director of Internships in the Office of Career Services at least one semester
prior to the planned internship and should submit a resume and complete all paperwork with the Office of
Career Services.
