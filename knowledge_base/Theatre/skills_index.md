## SECTION 1 — METADATA BLOCK

This index covers the Theatre Department at Southwestern University's Sarofim School of Fine Arts. Three degree paths are available: B.A. in Theatre Arts (35-36 credits, designed for students with a second major or multiple minors), B.A. in Theatre Performance and Production (39-45 credits, designed for focused theatre study), and a Minor in Theatre (5 courses). The department is housed in the Sarofim School of Fine Arts Building.

- Any question about a specific course: retrieve `Majoring___Minoring.txt` (to confirm if required) AND the relevant course document (`Design_Courses.txt`, `Performance_Courses.txt`, or `History_and_Literature_Courses.txt`)
- Any graduation requirement question: retrieve `Majoring___Minoring.txt` AND `SU_General_Policies.txt`
- Any study abroad or internship question: retrieve the specific program document (`New_York_Internships.txt`, `Internships_Abroad.txt`, or `Semester_in_London.txt`) AND `High_Impact_Learning_Experiences.txt` AND `SU_Resources_and_Financial.txt`
- Any funding question: retrieve `High_Impact_Learning_Experiences.txt` AND `SU_Resources_and_Financial.txt`
- Any ticket/performance question: retrieve `Box_Office.txt` AND `Ticket_Policies.txt`
- Any question about where shows are performed: retrieve `Current_Season.txt` AND `Facilities.txt`

**Degree path disambiguation triggers:**
- If student says "theatre major" or "the theatre degree" without specifying B.A. Theatre Arts vs. B.A. Theatre Performance and Production, ask: "Are you in the Theatre Arts B.A. (designed for double majors, 35-36 credits) or the Theatre Performance and Production B.A. (39-45 credits)? Or are you asking about the Theatre minor?"
- If student asks about elective requirements without specifying degree, disambiguation is needed before routing to elective sections

**Always escalate to human advisor:**
- Exceptions to the "independent study/internship credits cannot count toward major" rule
- Course substitution or waiver requests
- Academic appeals (probation, dismissal)
- Transfer credit evaluation for specific theatre courses
- Teacher certification in theatre (refer to Education Department)
- Any question about current-year costs for study abroad programs
- Departmental Honors requirements
- Production casting or audition scheduling

**Always retrieve `SU_General_Policies.txt` alongside major docs when:**
- Any graduation eligibility or credit count question
- Any general education requirement question
- Any grading policy question (Pass/D/F, grade appeals, incomplete grades)
- Any academic standing question (probation, dismissal, appeal)
- Any transfer credit or credit-by-examination question
- Any disability accommodation question (academic — not box office ADA seating)
- Any honors or distinction question (Latin Praise, Departmental Honors, Paideia with Distinction)
- Any double major or paired major policy question
- Teacher certification inquiry

**Always retrieve `SU_Resources_and_Financial.txt` alongside major docs when:**
- Any study abroad program question (deadlines, eligibility, credit transfer, costs)
- Any financial aid or tuition question
- Any refund or withdrawal-and-money question
- Any internship funding question beyond the Sarofim Travel Grant
- Any question about SURF or King Creativity Fund
