Discounted Tickets
Seniors (over 62), youth (under 16), students (with a valid high school or college ID), educators, and
the military have the option of discounted tickets.
Groups of 10
there is an automatic 15% discount.
Prices vary depending on performance, day, and ticket type.
ADA/Accommodated Seating
For accommodated seating (wheelchairs, walkers, no stairs) please call the box office for
reservations at (512) 863-1378.
Southwestern Community Tickets
All Southwestern students receive one complimentary ticket and faculty/staff receive two
complimentary tickets per production.
Many productions sell out. Please pick up your tickets at the Box Office at your earliest
convenience or follow the above instructions to purchase by phone.
Football Game Day Tickets
In addition, the Fine Arts Box Office location is not open on Saturdays either by phone or in-
person. We open one and half hours before games at Birkelbach Stadium.
