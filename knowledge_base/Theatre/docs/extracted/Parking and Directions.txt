Parking and Directions
Please do not hesitate to contact us about the location, however, please see
below for specifics of campus parking and a map.
Parking
Parking is free on thecampus and all parking lots are
available to our guests.
For the safety of yourself and others, utilize parking lots on thecampus when visiting the Sarofim School of Fine Arts. You can find parking at the Alma
Thomas Fine Arts Center, behind the Lois Perkins Chapel, and in other areas across
campus. Download a campus map here — parking areas indicated by a gold star can be used
for fine arts events.
Location
The Mathers Box Office is located in the Sarofim School of Fine Arts adjacent to the Alma
Thomas Theater.
Take IH35 exit 261 and turn right immediately onto University Ave. Continue on University
Ave for 1.6 miles and turn left into the parking lot on E. Rutersville Road. (This will be the
second left after you cross over the intersection of Maple St. and University.) You can take
either entrance to the Sarofim School of Fine Arts as the Box Office is in the middle of the
building.
