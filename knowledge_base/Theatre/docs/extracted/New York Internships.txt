HIGH IMPACT LEARNING EXPERIENCES
New York Internships
The Program’s setting in New York City affords students a first-hand
understanding of cultural diversity and a variety of perspectives and
conventions.
The New York Arts Program
is designed to help students acquire specific knowledge in their field
of interest while reinforcing the development of skills that support continued learning and
professional life such as the ability to work independently, establish priorities among tasks,
contribute to team work, manage time, communicate ideas, analyze and synthesize information,
and solve problems.
If you have a serious interest in the arts, creative industries and culture, the New York Arts
Program can introduce you to the world of established art and media professionals and
organizations in New York City; advance your creative and critical skills, and immerse you in a
diverse ecology of concepts and practices
The New York Arts Program offers: internships; seminars led by NYAP’s core faculty, and a range
of other creative professionals; individual advising by faculty to help you define learning goals and
explore career options in the arts and creative industries; participation in a community of students
and professionals representing a wide range of interests, skills and
backgrounds; program events designed to encourage interdisciplinary discussion and present a
comprehensive view of New York as a global cultural capital; and an independent urban living
experience in New York City. Students reside in the program’s five-story dormitory/office town
house in Chelsea, a residential area of Manhattan.
Applications are due in October and March for the following semester. Participants accepted: 50
per semester.
