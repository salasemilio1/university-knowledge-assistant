THEATRE
Facilities
The Theatre department is housed in the Sarofim School of Fine Arts
Building. Students have the opportunity to work and perform in three main
facilities.
Jones Theater
The Alma Thomas Theater
, a 750 seat proscenium theater.
The Jesse H. and Mary Gibbs Jones Theater
, a 325 seat thrust theater
The Heather Jean McGaughey Black Box
, a 50 seat performance and classroom space.
Students also have access to technical facilities, including the costume shop, scene shop, and
computer design studio- located throughout the Sarofim Fine Arts building.
