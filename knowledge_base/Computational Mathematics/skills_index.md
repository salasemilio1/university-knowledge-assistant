## SECTION 1 — METADATA BLOCK

This index covers all available documents for the Computational Mathematics program within the Mathematics/Computer Science (MAT/CSC) department at Southwestern University. The Computational Mathematics major is offered as both a B.A. (14 courses) and a B.S. (15 courses + 4 supporting science courses). The major blends core Mathematics and Computer Science courses to provide broad knowledge with depth in both disciplines. This index was generated from seven source documents covering degree requirements, course descriptions, placement guidance, faculty, student resources, extracurricular opportunities, and clubs/honor societies. Note: The MAT/CSC department also offers separate majors in Mathematics and Computer Science; this index focuses on Computational Mathematics. References to the Mathematics and Computer Science majors appear where relevant to shared courses and resources.
