SPANISH
Special Opportunities
Study Abroad
All students of Spanish are encouraged to study abroad. The Spanish department
offers programs for students at intermediate and advanced levels of Spanish. In addition, the
Office of Intercultural Learning has information about many other study and intern abroad
opportunities.
Sigma Delta Pi, Spanish Honor Society
Students majoring or minoring in Spanish are eligible to apply for membership to the Iota
Delta chapter of Sigma Delta Pi, the National Collegiate Hispanic Honor Society.
