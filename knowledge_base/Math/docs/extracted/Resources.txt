SCOPE: Summer Collaborative Opportunities and Experiences. Work with faculty members for 8
weeks during the summer on their research. Past projects have included an analysis of Choose-Your-

networks, and research in diagonalizing matrices.

bacterial colonies using photo input, and a wheelchair controlled by special glove. Deadline is in October.

research and creating works of art.
