Studio Art
1–2 minutes
At Southwestern, students have the opportunity to build both
technical and formal skills across a range of media, including
drawing, painting, sculpture, ceramics, and printmaking—
preparing them for graduate study and professional careers in the
arts. The Sarofim Fine Arts Gallery hosts professional exhibitions
and provides Studio Art majors with opportunities to showcase
their own artwork. Throughout their studies, students gain access
to exceptional opportunities—both locally and globally—that
broaden their perspectives and enrich their creative practice.
Since 2006 the Fiske Guide to Colleges has listedas among the top 25 small colleges and universities
strong in art or design.
“In a state where everything tends to be huge and overwhelming,
Southwestern proves that good things can – and do – come in
small packages.” –Fiske Guide to Colleges
