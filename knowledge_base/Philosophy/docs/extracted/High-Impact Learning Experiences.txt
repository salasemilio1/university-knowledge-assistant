PHILOSOPHY
High-Impact Learning Experiences
At Southwestern, high-impact experience are immersive opportunities that
position our graduates for active, meaningful lives of engagement and
achievement.
In addition to the multiple high-impact opportunities offered at Southwestern, Philosophy majors
also have the opportunity to engage in student research and/or be a part of the Philosophy club.
Student Research
Our current capstone students are busy working on a number of independent research projects. At
Southwestern, the capstone course for majors, or Colloquium, is conceived as a collaborative
workshop in which each student pursues an independent research project under the guidance of
the philosophy faculty as a whole.

Student Organization
Southwestern’s Philosophy Club is a student organization that promotes the discussion of current
philosophical topics and how club members can make a change in society starting at an everyday
level.
