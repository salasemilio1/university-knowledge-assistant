Southwestern students may study at the Intercollegiate Center for
Classical Studies in Rome
The Rome center is located on the Janiculum Hill overlooking downtown Rome The Center
offers courses in Latin, Greek, history, art history, and Italian.
Through a consortial agreement with the Associated Colleges of the South, students have
priority for admission and receive a tuition reduction. For information, please contact Dr.
Jeffrey Easton.
