### SU Universal Curriculum Requirements (General Education)

**Required for ALL degree paths:**
- First-Year Seminar (FYS) or Advanced-Entry Seminar (AES): 4 credits
- Languages and Cultures: through third-semester proficiency (up to 12 credits)
- Fitness and Recreational Activity (FRA): 1 credit
- Power and Justice (PJ) course: 1 course, 3-4 credits (cannot be satisfied by transfer credit)
- Exploration and Breadth: 6 courses from outside major (18-24 credits)
  - Part I: one course each from Fine Arts (FA), Humanities (H), Natural Sciences (NS), Social Sciences (ScS)
  - Part II: one additional course from two of the four areas

**Note for Classics students:** Many CLA07, LAT14, and GRK13 courses carry the (H) Humanities General Education designation. The Languages & Cultures requirement may be satisfied by completing Latin or Greek through the third-semester level (XX-164).

| Feature | B.A. Classics | B.A. Greek | B.A. Latin |
|---|---|---|---|
| Total courses | 9 | 8 | 8 |
| Greek required | Yes (4 courses, 1 upper) | Yes (7 upper + capstone) | No |
| Latin required | Yes (1 upper; 3 for lang. req.) | No | Yes (7 upper + capstone) |
| Classics in Translation | Yes (1 required from list) | No | No |
| Capstone | CLA07-964 | GRK13-954 | LAT14-954 |
| Foreign language requirement | Satisfied by 3 semesters Latin | Satisfied by Greek sequence | Satisfied by Latin sequence |
| Best for | Students wanting both languages + cultural breadth | Students focused on Greek literature/language | Students focused on Latin literature/language |
