## SECTION 1 — METADATA BLOCK

This index covers the Sociology major and minor within the Sociology & Anthropology Department at Southwestern University. The department offers a B.A. in Sociology (minimum 30 credits, eight courses) and a Minor in Sociology (minimum 18 credits, five courses). Note: the department also houses Anthropology majors and minors; this index focuses on the Sociology degree path but includes shared departmental resources. Key distinguishing features of this program include a mandatory cumulative research curriculum culminating in a qualitative/mixed-methods senior capstone, active student participation in regional and national conference presentations, faculty-student collaborative summer research (SURF/FSP), and the Texas Alpha Theta chapter of Alpha Kappa Delta (national sociology honors society). Four courses in the major must be taken at Southwestern and cannot be satisfied by transfer credit or other substitutions: SOC34-314, SOC34-344, SOC34-394, and SOC34-964.
