STUDENT RESEARCH AND AWARDS
Sociology National and Regional Awardssociology majors have won a number of national
and regional undergraduate paper awards. In 2006, we started nominating
student papers for national and regional awards. Since then, many of our
sociology majors have won the national Alpha Kappa Delta undergraduate
paper award (first, second, or third place), a number of our majors have won
the Southern Sociological Society’s Odum Award for best undergraduate
paper, and a few majors have received other outstanding undergraduate
paper awards.
