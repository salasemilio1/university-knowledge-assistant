SOCIOLOGY & ANTHROPOLOGY
For Current Students
There are numerous opportunities for Anthropology and Sociology majors to
get involved in research, internships, and study abroad experiences. Ask a
faculty member in the department about these options.
Summer Research Opportunities for Sociology and Anthropology Students
Work with faculty members in the department. Over the past five years, there have been
numerous faculty-student summer collaborative research groups that have conducted, presented,
and published studies together. Ask a faculty member in the department about these summer
opportunities (typically, students receive summer campus housing, a stipend, and funds to travel to
a professional meeting to present their research).
Research Training National Science Foundation – Research Experience for Undergraduates REU). In
the past, several of our sociology majors have completed the Demography REU at UT and several
anthropology majors have completed the Cultural Anthropology program at UNT. Texas A & M
currently offers a sociology REU experience.
Inter-university Consortium for Political and Social Research (ICPSR), Ann Arbor, MI offers a
summer program in quantitative methods for social research.
The Health Management and Policy Summer Enrichment Program at University of Michigan offers
an internship program for students interested in eliminating health disparities.
American Sociological Association Undergraduate Honors Program offers an introduction to
graduate school and careers in sociology as part of the annual conference each August.
