RELIGION
Majoring & Minoring
A Religion major prepares you well for graduate work in a number of liberal
arts fields (in addition to religion) and, for those seeking to pursue two
majors, it is also an excellent complementary major to the range of those
available at Southwestern. In addition, a Religion major is good for those
seeking to pursue an education in the seminary.
