Resources & Facilities
Art History classrooms meet the Fine Arts Building, the F.W. Olin Building,
the Roy and Lillie Cullen Building and the Charles and Elizabeth Prothro
Center for Lifelong Learning. You will End SU faculty and students
interacting in a variety of inspiring studios and intimate classrooms and
seminar spaces. Art History also makes extensive use of digital teaching
media (Moodle, chatrooms, and virtual exhibits of art works).
