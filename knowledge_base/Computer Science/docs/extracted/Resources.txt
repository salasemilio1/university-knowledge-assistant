OPPORTUNITIES AT SU
• King Creativity Fund - for creative projects. Groups of students
have used these funds for various projects, such as a 3D
interactive computer game to aid in the teaching of languages, and
a smartphone app to provide information about campus.
• SCOPE - Summer research for SU students. CS faculty often
participate in SCOPE, and faculty in other departments also
frequently have need of programmers to help in their research.
