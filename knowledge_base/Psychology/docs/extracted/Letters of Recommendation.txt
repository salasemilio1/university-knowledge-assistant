FOR CURRENT STUDENTS
Letters of Recommendation
In order to prepare an appropriate letter of recommendation, faculty members need to have
several important pieces of information from you (e.g., about the program/position for which
you’re applying, your grades, your strengths, previous experience, etc.).
Students seeking letters of ALL kinds (including letters for graduate school, on- or off-campus jobs,
internships, study abroad programs, and honor societies) are strongly encouraged to provide
faculty referees with this information.
After you have discussed the possibility of a faculty member serving as a reference, use the
document below to provide them with the requested information. Doing so will increase the
likelihood of getting a more informed, more in-depth reference.
