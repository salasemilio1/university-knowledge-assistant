PSYCHOLOGY
Internship Program
The Psychology Internship program is designed to provide students with
opportunities to work in real-world settings, to see first-hand the
application of psychological knowledge, and to explore potential career
paths.
Academic Internships can fulfill the capstone requirement or can be completed as elective credit.
Deadlines
Reserve a spot with an internship faculty mentor during course registration the PRIOR semester.
[Students who do not reserve a spot on time are not guaranteed an internship for the next
semester.]
