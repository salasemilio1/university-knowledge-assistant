Student Funding Opportunities
Rose Award
The maximum award per student is $600 per student, or 3 application fees (whichever is greater).
Applications are due on the last day of each month, and awards will be decided soon afterward.
Please consult with your Academic Adviser or with the Chair of the English Department (Michael
Saenger) if you think you may be eligible for this Award. Please note that Rose Application Fee
Awards will be made in the form of reimbursements, so remember to save receipts. You will be
required to submit a log of your time at the conference, and a reflection on your experience.
English Majors and Minors are eligible for this award.
